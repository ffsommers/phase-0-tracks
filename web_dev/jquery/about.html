About

I am an RC fenatic. Starting at the age of four I was obsessed with all things aviation and remote control. I started by building RC slope gliders with my father, but the obsession led me to establishing my own drone startup in San Francisco called AeriCam. We started with heavy-lift camera platform drones for the professional cinema industry. Most recently my obsession with drones focuses on the exciting growing sport of drone racing.
